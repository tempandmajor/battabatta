# Privacy Policy

Last reviewed July 2026. These policies may be updated as Battarbox grows.

Battarbox collects account information, profile content, posts, offers, messages, reports, approximate public location labels, private coordinates when users consent, and Stripe customer/payment references for platform support payments to OMS2.

Exact coordinates are private and must not be exposed through public profiles, public posts, discovery results, notifications, or message list APIs.

If ads are enabled, third-party vendors including Google may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar technologies to serve, measure, personalize, and protect ads. Google and its partners may use advertising cookies to serve ads based on a user's visits to Battarbox and other sites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ads Settings. If third-party ad serving beyond Google is enabled, Battarbox must disclose those vendors and applicable opt-out paths.

Users in regions requiring consent, including the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, must be given legally required disclosures and choices before personalized ad processing. Ads must remain disabled for those users until a Google-certified CMP is live.

Users can request account export and deletion by contacting support@battarbox.com. Some records may be retained or anonymized for security, legal, moderation, fraud prevention, or audit purposes.
