# Privacy Policy

Last reviewed July 2026. These policies may be updated as Battarbox grows.

Battarbox collects account information, profile content, posts, offers, messages, reports, approximate public location labels, private coordinates when users consent, and Stripe customer/payment references for platform support payments to OMS2.

Exact coordinates are private and must not be exposed through public profiles, public posts, discovery results, notifications, or message list APIs.

Users can request account export and deletion by contacting support@battarbox.com. Some records may be retained or anonymized for security, legal, moderation, fraud prevention, or audit purposes.
