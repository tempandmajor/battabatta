# Terms of Use Draft

This draft is implementation guidance and must be reviewed by counsel before unrestricted public usage.

BattaBatta helps adults coordinate non-binding barter discussions. Users are responsible for deciding whether, where, and how to meet or exchange goods or services.

BattaBatta does not provide tax, legal, safety, valuation, payment, escrow, shipping, settlement, completion accounting, or dispute-resolution services for user exchanges.

Payments made through Stripe support the platform and OMS2. They are not payments to another user for an exchange.
