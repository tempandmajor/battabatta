# Safety Policy

Last reviewed July 2026. These policies may be updated as Battarbox grows.

Battarbox is adult-only at launch.

Users should meet in public places, avoid sharing exact addresses until they trust the other party, and report suspicious or unsafe behavior.

The platform supports blocking, reporting, moderation review, account suspension, and post removal.

Battarbox does not verify professional licenses, insurance, permits, credentials, or legal eligibility to provide services. Members are responsible for conducting their own due diligence before participating in any exchange.

For non-emergency safety questions, contact support@battarbox.com.
