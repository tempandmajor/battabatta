# Prohibited Items And Services

Last reviewed July 2026. These policies may be updated as Battarbox grows.

Prohibited categories include illegal goods or services, weapons, firearms, ammunition, weapon accessories, controlled substances, cannabis, CBD, THC, drug paraphernalia, recalled products, counterfeit goods, adult sexual services or explicit sexual content, hazardous materials, stolen goods, medical or prescription items, alcohol, tobacco, gift cards, cash equivalents, money transfers, crypto, tokens, stored value, financial products, gambling, betting, raffles, sweepstakes, games of chance, hate or extremist content, and anything that creates an unreasonable safety, legal, or exploitation risk.

Battarbox may remove listings, restrict accounts, suspend accounts, block access, or report activity when listings violate policy.

Battarbox does not verify professional licenses, insurance, permits, credentials, or legal eligibility to provide services. Members are responsible for conducting their own due diligence before participating in any exchange.
