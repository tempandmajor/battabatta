# BattaBatta Product Requirements

## Goal

Help adults discover local or online non-binding barter opportunities while protecting privacy, safety, and nonprofit compliance.

## MVP Workflows

- Register, verify email, log in, log out, reset password, and use GitHub OAuth.
- Create a public profile with offers, interests, approximate location, and local/online preference.
- Pause offers with “not offering at the moment.”
- Publish offering and seeking posts.
- Restrict post availability with a total count, remaining count, unit label, and approval policy.
- Discover posts locally or online with category, goods/services, distance, and search filters.
- Save posts.
- Make an offer with offered item, requested item, timing, and note.
- Respond with interested, counter, decline, or withdraw.
- Message inside offer threads.
- Block and report users.
- Donate or become a supporter through Stripe Checkout.

## Non-Goals

- User-to-user payments.
- Escrow or settlement.
- Credits or balances.
- Completed-exchange accounting.
- Tax reporting automation for barter.
- Commercial seller marketplace behavior.
- AI-required posting.
